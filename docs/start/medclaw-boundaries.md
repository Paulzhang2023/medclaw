# MedClaw Boundaries

MedClaw is an open-source medical workflow product built on the OpenClaw architecture and released by MedSci (梅斯医学).

It is intended for trained medical, biomedical, and research professionals.

## Public disclaimer

MedClaw helps users collect, structure, and review medical information. It does not replace clinician judgment, formal guideline review, institutional policy, or patient-specific decision-making.

MedClaw outputs may be incomplete, outdated, or incorrectly extracted. Users must independently verify critical facts before clinical, regulatory, or publication use.

DeepEvidence and SeekEvidence are exposed as external entry points. Their outputs remain subject to their own product boundaries and should also be reviewed by qualified professionals.

## Responsible-use boundaries

- Do not present MedClaw as an autonomous diagnostic system.
- Do not present registry records as proof of efficacy without reviewing the actual evidence.
- Do not use the shared adapter registry as a general browsing directory.
- Remove or block unsafe, irrelevant, or policy-violating adapters from the registry.
- Keep a human review step for shared adapters and clinical summaries.
